Customer Onboarding V5

NIUM provides two integration paths for onboarding customers. The V5 API approach gives you full UX control, while the Pre-built Form offers faster, low-effort setup.

1.1 — Integration Options at a Glance

Scenario 1: V5 API (API Approach)

Recommended
IntegrationAPI-based
Engineering EffortHigher — full control over UX and flow
RFI TestingNot available in sandbox — use pre-built form for RFI testing
Best ForTailored onboarding; high-volume clients

Scenario 2: NIUM Pre-built Form

Alternative
IntegrationPre-built hosted form by NIUM
Engineering EffortLower — minimal engineering needed
RFI TestingSupported in sandbox
Best ForSmaller volumes; faster setup
